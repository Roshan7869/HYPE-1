# Page 28 — Privacy Policy (privacy.html)

## Purpose
Data privacy and protection policy — compliant with Indian DPDP Act 2023. Same layout system as Terms of Service for consistency.

## URL
`/privacy`

## Layout Structure
Same layout system as Terms of Service (27-TERMS). Hero, sticky TOC sidebar, content area. Only content changes.

## Content Sections

1. **Information We Collect** — Personal info (name, email, phone, address), payment data (via Cashfree, not stored), usage data, device info, cookies
2. **How We Use Your Information** — Order processing, communication, fraud prevention, analytics, marketing (with consent)
3. **Information Sharing** — Sellers (for order fulfillment), Cashfree (payments), analytics partners, law enforcement (when required)
4. **Data Security** — Encryption, secure storage, PCI-DSS compliance via Cashfree, regular audits
5. **Your Rights (DPDP Act)** — Access, correction, deletion, data portability, withdraw consent, grievance officer
6. **Cookies & Tracking** — Essential, analytics, marketing categories, opt-out instructions
7. **Data Retention** — How long data is kept, deletion timelines
8. **Children's Privacy** — Platform is 18+, no intentional collection from minors
9. **International Transfers** — Data processed in India, no cross-border transfer without consent
10. **Changes to Policy** — 30-day notice via email
11. **Contact & Grievance** — DPO email, grievance officer, registered address
